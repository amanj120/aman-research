the first 2020 democratic presidential debate wrapped up yesterday on the second night of the two part event .  here are key takeaways :
  senator kamala harris had a strong evening including a moment around race and identity where she pulled from personal history and directly challenged former vice president joe bidens record  see quote above  . 
  night two was more combative than night one with many of the candidates directly challenging each other and significantly more criticism of president trump . 
  emergent themes included generational appeals as younger candidates asserted that its time to pass the torch to solve a new generation of problems; gender appeals including closing the gap in female representation and protecting female reproductive rights; a broad political spectrum with moderate candidates challenging the feasibility of views from the most progressive candidates  e . g .  free college tuition and eliminating private health insurance  .
