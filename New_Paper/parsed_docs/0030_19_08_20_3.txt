a coalition of  20 states is planning to launch a big tech antitrust probe as early as next month according to the wall street journal .  the joint effort would enable states attorneys general to issue civil investigative demands  i . e .  subpoenas  to investigate whether large tech platforms are using market power to stifle competition .  the probe would be in addition to federal oversight efforts  already launched by the department of justice and ftc earlier this year .
