the trump administration announced changes to the endangered species act aiming to lift regulatory burdens created by the existing version of the 1973 law .  the changes include enabling officials to consider the economic cost while determining if a species warrants protection and ending the practice of giving threatened species  i . e .  one step away from endangered  the same protections as endangered species .  the act is credited for recovering species like the bald eagle grizzly bear and american alligator .
