the white house and congress announced a bipartisan two year deal that extends federal spending caps and lifts the government debt ceiling through july 2021  i . e .  past the 2020 elections  .  the deal will increase the federal budget deficit  approaching $1t annually  but avoids the previously looming possibility of a government shutdown or federal default later this year .  the measure is expected to be passed by the house this week and the senate next week  ahead of a summer recess  before heading to president trumps desk for final sign off .
