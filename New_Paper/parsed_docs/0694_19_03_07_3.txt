the european central bank  ecb  announced new stimulus measures in an effort to shore up a slowing eurozone economy amid global trade tensions brexit uncertainty and italian debt concerns .  the plan includes pushing out interest rate hikes until 2020 and offering banks cheap multi year loans .  the move is being called a reversal of policy after the ecb phased out its  $3t quantitative easing program in only december 2018  after  4 years  .
