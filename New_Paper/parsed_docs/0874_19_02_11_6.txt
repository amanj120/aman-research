finland released results from a universal basic income test after two years of running the trial .  key highlights:
  hypothesis: a guaranteed minimum basic income will help people find jobs . 
  test structure: give 2000 unemployed citizens a basic income of  $634 per month starting january 2017  instead of existing unemployment benefits  . 
  results: it improved the general "happiness" of participants but did not improve employment compared to a control group .
