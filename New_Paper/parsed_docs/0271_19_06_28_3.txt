the supreme court agreed to hear an appeal on ending daca during its next term .  three lower courts blocked a trump administration effort to end the deferred action for childhood arrivals  daca   program in 2017 .  the program protects  700k illegal immigrants brought to the us as children  known as dreamers  from deportation .  the next court term will start in october and end in june 2020 meaning a decision will come in the midst of election season  .
