china announced new 5 10% tariffs on $75b of us goods in retaliation to punitive tariffs on $300b of chinese goods announced by the us earlier this month .  china also said it will resume a 25% tariff on us vehicles and a 5% tariff on us automotive parts  paused in april  .  both the us and chinese tariffs will go into effect in two stages  on september 1 and december 15  and are expected to cover the remainder of any non tariffed goods .
