ongoing fires in the amazon rainforest have garnered international outcry including a call from french president macron  tweet   to discuss the international crisis at the g7 summit this weekend .  key figures behind the phenomenon include:
  fires in brazils amazon rainforest are up 84% year over year; however total fire activity across the amazon is near its 15 year average and below mid 2000s figures  . 
  amazon fires are primarily caused by deliberate human action ranging from small scale agriculture to mechanized deforestation . 
  deforestation was 278% higher in july 2019 compared to july 2018 . 
   60% of the amazon is in brazil; approximately 20% of its biome has already been lost over the last 50 years  primarily due to deforestation .
