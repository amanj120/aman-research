the g7 held its annual summit in biarritz france over the weekend .  the g7 includes canada france germany italy japan the uk and the us and represents  58% of global wealth  more   .  key highlights included:
  the us and japan agreed in principle on a trade deal that president trump and japanese prime minister shinzo abe plan to sign at the un general assembly in new york next month  . 
  the us and france reached a draft compromise on a new french digital tax that disproportionately affects large us based tech platforms  .  president trump previously threatened a retaliatory tax on french wine . 
  the us pushed to readmit russia to the group  with support from italy but opposition from most other g7 leaders  .  president trump argued it should be a part of discussions related to iran syria and north korea .
