saudi arabia launched its first visa program for foreign tourists  from 49 countries  amid a broader push to decrease its economic dependence on oil  .  the kingdom hopes tourism will grow to 10% of gdp by 2030  from 3% today  .  saudi also said it would allow women to travel unaccompanied and relaxed strict dress requirements .  previously visas were generally limited to business people expats and religious pilgrims .
