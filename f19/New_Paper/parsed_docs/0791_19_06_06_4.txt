youtube tightened its hate speech policy in an effort to reduce the prevalence of harmful content on its platform .  key updates include:
  prohibiting videos alleging group superiority to justify discrimination  e . g .  content promoting or glorifying nazi ideology  . 
  removing content that denies well documented violent events such as the holocaust or the shooting at sandy hook elementary . 
  reducing the spread of borderline content and increasing authoritative content through smarter video recommendation systems and tighter monetary incentives  i . e .  removing monetary incentives for accounts that repeatedly toe the line  .
