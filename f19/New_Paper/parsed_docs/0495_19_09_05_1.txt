the uk house of commons passed a bill designed to block a no deal brexit on october 31  the current withdrawal deadline  .  it now goes to the house of lords and is expected to pass by end of day friday .  the bill requires prime minister boris johnson to seek a 3rd brexit delay from the eu  for three months  in the absence of a negotiated deal .  also yesterday uk lawmakers rejected a subsequent call from johnson for a snap general election on october 15 .
