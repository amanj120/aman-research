italian prime minister conte announced his resignation yesterday after 14 months of leading a coalition government  as an independent prime minister  .  conte accused his interior minister  matteo salvini  of manufacturing a government crisis in search of a pretext to return to the polls after salvinis right wing party surged in eu elections  in may  .  italian president mattarella will now work with party leaders to explore forming a new coalition government .  if unsuccessful mattarella will dissolve parliament and call early elections  .
