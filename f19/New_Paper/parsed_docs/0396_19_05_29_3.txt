the supreme court turned down an appeal  7 2  to reinstate an indiana abortion law upholding a lower court ruling .  the law signed in 2016 by then governor mike pence gave the state power to prohibit abortions motivated on the basis of sex race or disability .  by not taking up the case the supreme court effectively avoided making a major decision on the constitutional right to abortion  i . e .  reconsidering roe v .  wade  .
