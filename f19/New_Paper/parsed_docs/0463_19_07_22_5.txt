china contributed the most companies to the 2019 fortune global 500 list for the first time taking 129 spots  compared to 121 for the us  .  the list is an annual ranking of the top 500 corporations worldwide by revenue .  the us still led in total revenue contribution  i . e the largest companies on the list tended to be us based  accounting for 29% of total revenue  compared to 26% for china  .
