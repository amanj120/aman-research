european parliament elections concluded on sunday after four days of voting .  key takeaways based on current estimates include:
  pro eu parties held onto a solid majority . 
  the two largest traditional center left and center right parties lost their 40 year held combined majority  meaning they will need to form more new alliances with smaller parties  . 
  populist and far right parties gained ground in some countries  incl .  italy and france  but fell short of windfall victories . 
  voter turnout was 51% the highest in 20 years and reversing a declining trend .
