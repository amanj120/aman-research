the white house dropped efforts to add a citizenship question to the 2020 census yesterday .  the announcement followed two weeks of conflicting developments around the controversial question including:
  two weeks ago the supreme court blocked the question citing a lack of reasoned explanation by the administration for its addition  as required under law  but did not rule the question itself unconstitutional . 
  early last week the commerce department announced it initiated the census printing process  a massive effort  without adding the question . 
  mid last week president trump said the commerce department would continue its efforts to add the question  tweet   with assistance from the justice department in finding an alternate legal basis . 
  early this week two federal judges blocked an effort by the administration to swap its legal teams in outstanding  citizenship question related  us district court cases .
